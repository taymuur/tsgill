An amoral and cunning guide to power dynamics drawn from 3,000 years of history. Robert Greene distills the essence of power into 48 concise laws, illustrated with historical examples from figures like Sun Tzu, Napoleon, and Machiavelli.

Currently reading this fascinating exploration of power, strategy, and human nature. Each law provides timeless wisdom on navigating the complex dynamics of power in personal and professional life.
