The most comprehensive and authoritative textbook on artificial intelligence. This fourth edition covers the latest developments in AI, including deep learning, probabilistic programming, multiagent systems, and robotics.

Russell and Norvig provide an excellent foundation for understanding AI principles, algorithms, and applications. The book balances theoretical concepts with practical implementations, making it an invaluable resource for both students and practitioners in the field.

Key topics include intelligent agents, problem-solving, knowledge representation, planning, machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. A must-read for anyone serious about understanding artificial intelligence.
