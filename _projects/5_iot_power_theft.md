Led development of an intelligent IoT-based system addressing Pakistan's Rs. 90 billion annual electricity theft problem. The system uses advanced sensor networks, Raspberry Pi processing, and cloud-based monitoring through ThingSpeak to detect both line tampering and meter tampering in real-time.

### Key Achievements

- Led team of **6 undergraduate students** to successful prototype development
- Published research achieving **18 citations** - demonstrating high impact
- Developed real-time monitoring system with **< 1 second response time**
- Created **cost-effective solution** scalable to grid-level deployment
- Addressed critical infrastructure problem affecting entire nation

### Technical Approach

The system comprises:

- **Sensor Network** - PZEM-004T high-precision sensors with 0.5% accuracy
- **Edge Computing** - Raspberry Pi for real-time processing
- **Cloud Platform** - ThingSpeak for scalable data management
- **Detection Algorithms** - Threshold-based and anomaly detection
